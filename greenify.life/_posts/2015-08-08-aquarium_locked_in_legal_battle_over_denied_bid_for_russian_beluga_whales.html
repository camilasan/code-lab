--- title: "Aquarium locked in legal battle over denied bid for Russian beluga whales" created_at: 2015-08-08 16:39:18 UTC author: The Guardian Environment layout: post ---

Georgia Aquarium argues introducing new belugas into the captive population in the US would diversify the gene pool, but NOAA Fisheries denied their request

The Georgia Aquarium is locked in a legal battle with a federal agency over the denial of its request to bring 18 beluga whales from Russia for display in aquariums in the US.
